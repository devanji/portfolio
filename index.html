I am a dedicated and passionate graduate with a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science from NSBM Green University, Homagama. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a strong foundation in UI/UX design and UX engineering, combining theoretical knowledge with practical application to create intuitive, efficient, and visually engaging user-centered digital experiences.

To further strengthen my expertise, I completed a Web Designing program at the University of Moratuwa, gaining hands-on experience in functional and creative interface design. I also pursued an AI/ML course at SLIIT University, driven by my interest in emerging technologies and their potential to enhance user experience through intelligent and adaptive digital solutions.

In addition to my academic and technical background, I am a Co-Founder and Director of Novalink Innovations (Pvt) Ltd, where I actively contribute to product strategy, design, and innovation. Currently, I am pursuing a Master of Technology (Professional Computing) at Swinburne University of Technology, with a strong commitment to continuous learning and applying my multidisciplinary skills to develop impactful and innovative digital solutions.
